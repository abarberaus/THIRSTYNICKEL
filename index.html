A Little About Us

Built in the 1870's, Thirsty Nickel provides a combination of bars, stages and rooms to provide something for everyone. From our 4,500 square foot sprawling entry to our Buffalo Room overflowing with Distressed Hardwood, authentic architectural salvage furniture and a custom handmade bar, Thirsty Nickel truly captures the full essence of Austin.

Located in Austin's Historic Entertainment District, Thirsty Nickel is truly a "must see" for guests from all over the world. Whether you are here on business or pleasure, take a moment to see what Austin is truly all about.... live music, craft cocktails and private events like nowhere else. Oh, if these walls could talk.....
