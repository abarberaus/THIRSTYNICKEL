A Little About Us

Built in the 1870's, Thirsty Nickel provides a combination of bars, stages and rooms which range from our 4,500 square foot sprawling entry to our Buffalo Room which is overflowing with Distressed Hardwood, authentic architectural salvage furniture and a custom handmade bar that truly captures the full essence of Austin

Located in Austin's Historic Entertainment District, Thirsty Nickel is truly a "must see" for guests from all over the worldWhether you are here on business or pleasure, take a moment to see what Austin is truly all about.... live music, craft cocktails and private events like nowhere else.
